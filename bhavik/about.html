About US

A very well-established MOT garage, car service garage and car repair garage in Leicester- Moto services carry out Servicing and Repairs to Range Rover and Volkswagen vehicles. We can offer the same service as a main dealer at a fraction of the price. We have comprehensive knowledge across the board together with all the manufacturer resources for all makes and models. When using the services at Moto services, you can rest assured that your car is in the best possible place. We have built up an enviable reputation for servicing, repairs and MOTs in Leicester and surrounding area with our customers who will tell you the service you receive with us is second to none, whilst maintaining the highest quality of work. Here at Moto services you can be assured of effective problem solving and attention to detail and our goal is to meet and exceed the expectations of every customer.
